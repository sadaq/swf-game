Title: No Mutants Allowed

Width: 640

Height: 530

Tags: Shooting, Defense, Action

Description:
No Mutants Allowed is a challenging, frantic, fast-paced action game! After a post-nuclear breakout, mutants are emerging from their depths, crawling to the surface and rampaging across the desert towards the city! Defend your base, using your best shooting skills, building up a small army, and learning new abilities. The safety of the city is on your shoulders!

Instructions:
Aim and fire - Move the mouse cursor over an enemy.
Special attacks - 1, 2, and 3 numbers keys
