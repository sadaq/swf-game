Description:
New York Taxi License is a driving game where you learn to drive a taxi in New York! Follow the instructor and drive safe in order to get your ticket to a new job.  This game has a scoreboard, so the quicker you are the higher up the scoreboard you'll be. But by careful as rushing can cause a crash and you'd fail.Great practice for those actually about to do their tests! 

Instructions:
Arrow keys to drive
