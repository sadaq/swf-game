Title: Lucky Balls

Width: 560

Height: 420

Tags: Matching, Marble, Puzzle

Description:
Lucky Balls is a colorful Puzzle Game. The aim is to eliminate all the colorful balls rolling down the spiral tube, before they reach the skull. You can eliminate them by shooting a ball from the center and group 3 or more balls of the same color.

The degree of difficulty rises as you proceed further.

Instructions:
Move your Mouse to set the direction and Left Click to shoot a ball. Good Luck and enjoy...
