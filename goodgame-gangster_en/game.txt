Title: Goodgame Gangster

Width: 810

Tags: Simulation, Gangster, Multiplayer, MMO, Management

Description:
Goodgame Gangster is a simulation game in which you play a gangster. Complete missions for the godfather to gain rewards and improve your character, then duel with other real players and buy new items from the black market, in time you will be able to build up your own family and make it the most powerful in the city,

Instructions:
Use the mouse to control the action in game.
