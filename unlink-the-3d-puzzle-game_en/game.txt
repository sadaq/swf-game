Title: UnLink - The 3D Puzzle Game

Width: 800

Height: 600

Tags: 3D, Mouse, Skill, Puzzle

Description:
UnLink is a simple and addictive 3D puzzle game.

The goal in this game is simply to "UnLink" 3D shapes from each other, but what starts out easy becomes some fiendishly challenging and difficult! Master the 23 Level to prove your unlinking skills.

Made with a beautiful combination of different colours Unlink will put your skills to the test as you try to unlink 3D shapes from each other. 

Instructions:
Mouse = Drag Shapes...
Hold the "Shift Key" + Mouse = Rotate The Camera Angle
