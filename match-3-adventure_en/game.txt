Title: Match 3 Adventure

Width: 640

Height: 610

Tags: Logic, Skill, Puzzle, Scoreboard, Action, Fighting, Physics

Description:
In this Anime-style puzzle game, you'll have to match up three or more blocks of the same color to clear the board and kill the baddies! Getting a higher number of color combinations allows your character to use more powerful attacks. Take a variety of new and challenging monsters in this unique new puzzle game!

Instructions:
Use the mouse to move the blocks just like Bejeweled.
