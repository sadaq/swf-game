Description:
In Getaway, you and your fellow criminals have just committed a heist and have made away with a ton of loot!  It's up to you to get them to their destination safely, but the police won't make it easy for you!  Dodge and weave your way to victory and make the perfect getaway with your bag full of loot?.

Instructions:
· Accelerate - Up arrow key.
· Decelerate - Down arrow key.
· Turn left/right - Left/right arrow keys respectively.
