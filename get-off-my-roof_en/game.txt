Title: Get Off My Roof

Width: 640

Height: 480

Tags: Scoreboard, Arcade, Shooting, Action

Description:
Get Off My Roof is an Arcade Shooting Game in which you must try and use rocks and other weapons like newspapers, brooms etc to knock pigeons off your roof! You must at the same time avoid being hit by their toxic poo. You can clean yourself by using the Q button. You earn money for every pigeon you knock off the roof, use this money to purchase new destructive weapons.

Instructions:
Controls:
Left Mouse: Throw Projectile
Spacebar: Jump
A: Move Left
D: Move Right
W: Swing Melee Weapon
Q: Use Wetnap (Heal one heart)
[1-2]: Switch Projectile Weapon

P: Pause
