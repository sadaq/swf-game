Title: Rocket Rex

Width: 600

Height: 450

Tags: Upgrade, Dinosaur, Unity, Distance

Description:
Rocket Rex is a fun endless game where you are a dinosaur with a rocket attached to your back! What could be better?! Rockets AND Dinosaurs!
Fly your way as far as you can and collect as many eggs as possible. Use your eggs to buy bonuses and a new dinosaur!
Guaranteed endless fun with Rocket Rex!
- Fun For All Ages
- Control Your Very Own Rex!
- Fly Further Than Any Dinosaur Has Flown Before!

Instructions:
Click and hold mouse to use rocket to rise, release to descend.
