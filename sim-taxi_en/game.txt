Title: Sim Taxi

Width: 640

Height: 480

Tags: RPG, Racing, Car, Taxi, Simulation, 3D

Description:
In Sim Taxi, the main objective of the game is to look for passengers and deliver them to their destinations as quickly as you possibly can but without damaging your car. A phone operator will help you locate customers but also you don't need to worry where the locations are becasuse there's a compass on the top left to guide you.

&amp;middot; &lt;b&gt;Switch Radio Stations&lt;/b&gt; = 1,2,3 Keys
