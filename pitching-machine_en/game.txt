Title: Pitching Machine

Width: 560

Height: 420

Tags: Baseball, Simulation, Sports

Description:
Pitching Machine is a Baseball Batting Simulator. You must try to hit all 10 balls as far as you can. (before you can start, you must select a team). Click the Start Button from the top left. Hold the bat and cick the mouse when you want to swing the bat. The pitching machine will show a red light when the ball is about to be released. Also you can select your own choice of bat, and adjust the speed of the ball on the speedometer.
