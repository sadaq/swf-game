Title: Om Nom Zombies

Width: 670

Height: 442

Tags: Zombie, Shooting, War, Action, Aiming, Army, Scoreboard

Description:
In this unique new arcade-style zombie shooter, you'll have to defend the beautiful, helpless woman from the oncoming zombies who are looking for a midnight snack. You'll only be able to see a small area thanks to your flashlight, but watch the battery life! Your sight will become more limited as the batteries die. Grab more batteries, ammo, and even cool weapons like a bomb you can strap onto zombies!

Instructions:
Use the mouse to aim and shoot
