Title: Tower 100

Width: 640

Height: 480

Tags: Balance, Puzzle, Physics

Description:
Tetris meets a tower builder in this hybrid of Tower 100. You're given 100 blocks and are commanded to build the tallest tower you can. You're provided a small platform in which to base your foundation. Each block has its own physics dimension, and may require more focus than just dropping a block. Make sure your tower is well balanced, using careful planning and thought. Can you create the perfect tower?
