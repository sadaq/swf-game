Title: Russian in the Army

Width: 550

Height: 400

Tags: Strategy, Killing, Army

Description:
In Russian in the Army you are an agent sent to plant a time bomb precisely to inflict as much damage to the Russian army as possible. The bomb will set off after 5 seconds so be careful where and when you plant it. A tip to winning the game is to look out for a clusters of enemy personnel required for the mission and predict when they will intersect. Killing high ranking officers give you extra points.

Instructions:
&amp;middot; &lt;b&gt;Move Left / Right&lt;/b&gt; = Left or Right Arrow Keys
&amp;middot; &lt;b&gt;Plant Bomb&lt;/b&gt; = Space Bar
