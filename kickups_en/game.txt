Title: KICKUPS

Width: 500

Height: 450

Tags: Sports, Football, Soccer

Description:
The aim of this game is to keep the football up in the air for as long as possible. The longer you keep it in the air the higher your score will be. This is not your average football game, there are no time limits or opponents or goals to score. This game is best enjoyed when played against a friend. So challenge your friends by sharing this game with them. Fans of the great Brazilian Ronaldinho, this game is for you.

Instructions:
&middot; Juggle The Ball = Use Mouse
