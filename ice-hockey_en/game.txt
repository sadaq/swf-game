Title: Ice Hockey

Width: 650

Height: 480

Tags: Sports, Hockey

Description:
Ice Hockey is a sports game. Take control of your three team members and score as many goals as you can. There is a three minute time limit. You can carry the puck yourself or pass it to a team member. Every member can move around the rink even the goalkeeper. You will be able to automatically control your player closest to the puck.

Instructions:
&amp;middot; &lt;b&gt;Movements&lt;/b&gt; = Arrow keys
&amp;middot; &lt;b&gt;Hit / Push&lt;/b&gt; = Space Bar
