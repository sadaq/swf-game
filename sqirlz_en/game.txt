Title: sqirlz

Width: 640

Height: 460

Tags: Skill, Scoreboard, Puzzle

Description:
Gravity can be your best friend or your worst enemy! In this innovative new physics-puzzle game, you'll have to help Sqirlz -- our squishy hero -- reach his jelly platform at the base of every structure by clicking on the red blocks that stand in the way you will eliminate them and give Sqirlz a route to the bottom. Using your wits, speed, and the power of gravity, solve each of these creative new puzzles to advance to the next stage!

Instructions:
Use the mouse to remove the blocks.
