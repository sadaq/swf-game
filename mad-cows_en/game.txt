Title: Mad Cows

Width: 550

Height: 400

Tags: Cow, Funny, Animal, Shooting

Description:
In This Farming Game, you find out the true but sad fact that your cows have been infected by a strange disease. They are getting mad. Your only chance is to pick out the infected animals and shoot them. You have 60 seconds to find 30 mad cows.  Note: A healthy cow is behaving exactly like a cow should do. So avoid these as it'll cost you 10 seconds off the clock for putting a healthy cow down. Precision is everything in this quick clicker skill game.

Instructions:
&middot; Play This Game = Use Mouse
