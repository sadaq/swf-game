Title: Sky Knight 2

Width: 640

Height: 570

Tags: Flying, Aircraft, Airplane, Shooting

Description:
In Sky Knight 2, it's make or break as you take on the menacing sky battalion, armed only with your plane, your guns, and if you're lucky... a friend.  Be a one-man air army, or team up with a fellow sky knight to defeat the unrelenting enemy force.  Either way, you'll be outnumbered, so stay sharp!

Instructions:
To move: W,A,S,D keys or mouse (two-player).
To fire: M or Left Mouse Button
Use bomb: N or Enter key.
Pause game/shop: Spacebar
