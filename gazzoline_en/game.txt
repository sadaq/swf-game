Title: Gazzoline

Tags: Point and Click, Parking, Car, Strategy, RPG, Business

Description:
Do you have what it take to run a gas station on your own in Gazzoline? Test your pumping skills in this game and become the ultimate fuelling machine. This game will test your reflexes and your customer service skills. You are the only attendant at the Gazzzoline fuelling station. Your goal is to make a fortune by giving good service to your clients. Try to serve as many customers as you possibly can, without upsetting them. As you begin your game, you will be provided with in game instructions. Follow these instructions for hassle free game play.
